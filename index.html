About TEDx CUSAT

TEDxCUSAT is an independently organized TEDx event hosted by the students of the Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) in Kochi, Kerala, India. Founded in 2019, the organization operates under a free license from TED to bring together innovative minds to share ideas worth spreading, with a mission to inspire meaningful change and transform concepts into real-world actions.
